Benefits of Annuities

Annuities are an excellent retirement and investment vehicle. With so many different types to choose from, you may find a great annuity that lowers your taxes and provides a secure financial future.

Tax-Deferred Growth

You will pay no income taxes on the earnings from your annuity investments until you begin making withdrawals or receiving periodic payments.

Unlimited Contributions

Generally speaking, there is no limit to the amount of after-tax money you can put into an annuity, regardless of your income level.

Investment Choices

Fixed annuities offer a stated rate of treturn while variable annuities include a variety of investment options.

No Mandatory Withdrawals

If your annuity is not part of an IRA or a qualified retirement plan, you are not required to take minimum distrubtions.

Death Benefit

In general, your payments are guaranteed to your beneficiaries if you die before withdrawals begin.

Lifetime Income Benefits

You can receive annuity payments over the course of your life.

Volatility Protection

Many annuities offer protection against market swings.

Growth Potential

When used strategically, annuities can offer significant returns to boost your portfolio.
